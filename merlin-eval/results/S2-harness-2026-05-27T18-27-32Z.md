### Defect 2: `total_for()` returns everything *except* the requested category

### Defect 3: `lookup_total()` panics on unknown category

- **Root cause**: `ledger/src/lib.rs:61` — `.unwrap()` on `Option::None` when a category has no entries (fresh HashMap lookup).

### Defect 4: `parallel_total()` never joins threads (race condition)

- **Root cause**: `ledger/src/lib.rs:83-88` — `thread::spawn` handles were discarded; the function acquired the lock and returned while worker threads were still running or before they'd written their partial sums. This produced non-deterministic results.
